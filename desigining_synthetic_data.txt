## Designing the Synthetic Data


Feature Type	                                Features
1. Claim Details	            Claim ID, Claim Amount, Procedure Code, Service Date, Claim Status
2. Patient Information	        Patient ID, Age, Gender, Location, Medical History, Insurance Plan Type
3. Provider Information	        Provider ID, Specialization, Location, Reputation Score
4. Financial Metrics	        Payment Method, Time Between Claims, Cost Deviation
5. Fraud Indicators	            Fraudulent (binary label), Suspicious Timing, Duplicate Claims
